General Procedure

Kate's 'Synthesis-Directed Elaborations'

  1. Two fragment screen hits are merged with Fragmenstein
  2. The closest catalog compound is used as a base for enumeration. Six target compounds identified:
    • D2R-5
    • D2R-12
    • Ric8inhib-1
    • Ric8inhib-3
    • Ric8enhan-3
    • CB1R-5
  3. The base compound is split into two building blocks
  4. The building blocks are elaborated to create many different reaction products

Max's 'Hit Interaction Profiling for Procurement Optimisation'

  1. A 'HIPPO' object is prepared with the fragment screen hits, follow-up compounds with their reaction paths and purchasing metadata
  2. The protein-ligand interactions are fingerprinted
  3. Many random collections of products and their associated building blocks are generated
  4. These building block sets are scored and ranked

Random BBS Constraints

  • $8000 total budget
  • Lead time at most 35 working days
  • Building blocks at most $100/mg

Scoring Metrics

  • The total number of products
  • Hit features coverage
  • New protein features (half weight)
  • Average Fragmenstein ddG (quarter weight)
